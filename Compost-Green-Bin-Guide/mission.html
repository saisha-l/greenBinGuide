From the Ground Up.

The future of tomorrow lies right in the dirt beneath our feet.

In landfills, plastic can take up to 500 years to decompose. Composting can get the job done in just a few months.

Did you know? Organic food waste that is mistakenly placed in landfills is responsible for 58% of landfill methane emissions. Composting not only helps reduce the size of our landfills, but also helps battle climate change.
